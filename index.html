Linotte
Goal
Create as many alignments as possible (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
How to play
On your turn, roll 5 dice. You may roll up to three times, keeping any dice you want after each roll and re-rolling the others.
If you make a valid combination, place one of your tokens on one and just one matching empty space on the board. Then the turn passes to the next player.
If no combination is made after three rolls, your turn ends.
You are never forced to place a token.
The game ends when a player has placed all their tokens or completes a line of five tokens
Highest score wins.
